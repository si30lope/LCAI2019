LET'S CHAT ABOUT IT

Welcome! We're LET'S CHAT ABOUT IT. Our goal is to create a judgment-free space for users to feel comfortable about opening up and to have someone there to listen. We are a startup actively developing a wellness support platform and community to share, support, collaborate, and gain confidence to combat substance abuse and to be able to live life with mental wellness.

A Wellness Support Platform

At Let's Chat About It, we believe that connection & community are the core of health. Whether it be Mental Health or Substance Abuse; Peer Support or Health Care Professionals, we believe having access to care at the most critical times is essential for wellness.
